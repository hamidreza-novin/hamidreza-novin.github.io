Bio

Hamidreza Novin received his ​B.Sc. degree in electrical engineering (electronics),at Tabriz University in 1400, and is currently studying for a ​M.Sc. degree in electrical and electronic engineering (IC), at Sharif University of Technology.
He is also an alternate member of the Islamic Society of Engineers.
In addition to studying, he is also interested in artistic and sports activities and has honors in the field of kung fu.
He also deals with cultural activities.
